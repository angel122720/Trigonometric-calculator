Quotient

tan θ = sin θ / cos θ
cot θ = cos θ / sin θ

Reciprocal

csc θ = 1 / sin θ
sec θ = 1 / cos θ

Pythagorean

sin²θ + cos²θ = 1
1 + tan²θ = sec²θ

Co-Function

sin(π/2 - θ) = cos θ
cos(π/2 - θ) = sin θ
